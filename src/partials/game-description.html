Game Description

  • Welcome to Match Stars, where classic match-3 puzzles meet the power of unique star characters!

  • Switch, swap, and blast through colorful levels while using special abilities from your Match Stars team.

  • Each character has their own skills to help clear tiles, create explosive combos, and complete tricky puzzles.

  • With hundreds of levels, exciting challenges, and powerful boosters, this is the ultimate match-3 adventure.

  • Collect rewards, progress through unique worlds, and become a Match Stars champion!
